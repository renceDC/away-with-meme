Mission

To establish a stigma-free community to educate students and parents about the damaging effects by taking positive action against and work its prevention. We aim to achieve the milestone in serving high-quality entertainment and informative lessons about mental health issues to the youth, providing financial help to the unfortunate.

Vision

To halt the stigma by producing several online sources and furnish online content that aligns with our advocacy and beliefs that resides in Away with Meme. Thus, it will help us to promote our campaign in educating others and ourselves for better perspectives.

Purpose

The foremost objective of Away with meme is to have a widespread community of positivity in the view of memes and/or viral videos, inspirational quotes, motivational messages to the youth who are having a hard time battling weariness in which we will raise awareness to always prioritize mental health amongst ourselves.

• to reach out to people who live in despair and give them proper practice of coping mechanisms as we promote stress-free lifestyle.
