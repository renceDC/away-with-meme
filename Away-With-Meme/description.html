About Our Website

•  The Away with meme describes itself as a page that promotes positivity and inspiration amidst these challenging events. The page advocates matters related to mental health and raising awareness: adolescents are primarily the demographic.

• The group's primarily objective is to create a website to post a blog and a Facebook page to raise awareness about this issue. • Our group attempts to establish a stigma-free community to educate students and parents about the damaging effects by taking positive action against and working for its prevention. We aim to achieve the milestone in serving high-quality entertainment and informative lessons about mental health issues to the youth, providing financial help to the unfortunate.

• The main course of action is to halt the stigma by producing several online sources and furnish online content that aligns with our advocacy and beliefs that resides in Away with meme. Thus, it will help us to promote our campaign in educating others and ourselves for better perspectives.
